Our Story

Established in 2022 in Finland, Pidin Cardholder Wallets are rooted in customer-centric design and innovation. We draw inspiration from our customers' needs and lifestyles, ensuring each product not only offers security but also aligns with contemporary aesthetics and functionality. Our commitment to innovation is reflected in our range of high-quality wallets and Cardholder, available on Amazon, featuring discreet RFID protection and a variety of styles for any preference. With a focus on durability, elegance, and innovative design, SafeGuard Wallets delivers peace of mind and sophistication for the modern consumer.
